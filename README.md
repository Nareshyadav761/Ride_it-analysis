# Ride_it-analysis

****Overview****

A quick analysis of a ride-hailing dataset to understand how customers book rides, why cancellations happen, and how driver performance affects overall operations.

****Key Numbers****

25M ride offers

7M bookings

6M completed rides

37K active drivers

76% of rides are PHV

Average rating: 4.89

****Main Insights****

Low conversion: Only 24% of requests turn into completed rides.

PHV dominates: It drives most of the trips, so optimising PHV has the highest impact.

High passenger cancellations: Mainly due to long wait times or sudden fare changes.

Driver imbalance: Most drivers work in PHV, making taxis less available.
